-about us

The study titled "Knowledge Level in the Use of Microsoft Excel Application among Selected Bachelor of Science in Office Administration Students" by the Researchers has produced an e-guide aimed at improving the competency of students in using Microsoft Excel. These students are those who are currently taking Integrated Software. The beneficiaries of this e-guide are the students themselves who will benefit greatly from its contents in enhancing their knowledge and skills in using Microsoft Excel.
